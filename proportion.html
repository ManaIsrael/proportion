Major Sara Fekadu

Major Sara F. Yalew, Born on April 19, 1968 E.C., From her mom Gabriel, and her dad Fekadu, in Adwa, Tigrai. She has a husband named Yemane and a daughter named Betelhem, and a son, Ermias. She lives in Addis Ababa, Ethiopia, with her children.

Sara was the first and the last child for her mom and dad. She grew up with her dad and grandmother, Amlesu. She started school at a very young age and joined the army force at grade 10 and had to stop school for several years. She then married dad in 1987 E.C. and bore my brother on April 24, 1988 E.C. and bore me on January 11, 1990 E.C. she continued her journey of school from there and studied different kinds of fields and got different kinds of Certificates.
